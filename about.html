ABOUT BULGOGI BITES

Bulgogi Bites is a black owned restaurant that was established in 2026 by a Motswana student who had grown fond of the Korean culture. The restaurant was first established with the aim of making Korean immigrants feel closer to home through the comfort and warmth our food offers. With the growing number of customers Bulgogi Bites has proven to be more than that, it has now become the number 1 tourist destination. Along with Korean inspired dishes the restaurant is best known for infusing its food with Japanese herbs and vegetables.Our mission is to give our customers a taste of Korea with food that feeds not the body but the soul

Our restaurant offers Korean meals, desserts and drinks made with love just for you. From the moment you set foot in our restaurant you are greeted by our top notch staff who prioritise your comfort above anything else.Our restaurant makes you feel, taste and see the Korean culture.
